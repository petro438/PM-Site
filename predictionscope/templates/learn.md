concepts to a mainstream audience.

## Guidelines

- Always explain jargon the first time it appears
- Use "you" to address the reader directly
- Include at least one specific example with real market data
- Link to other /learn/ articles for related concepts
- Link to /best/ articles when mentioning specific platforms
- End with a clear next step or call to action
