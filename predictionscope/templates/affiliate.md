prediction market platforms with affiliate monetization.

## Guidelines

- Always include affiliate disclosure at the top
- Always include risk disclosure at the bottom
- Be genuinely honest about cons — trust converts better than hype
- Never fabricate promo codes or offers
- If a promo field is empty, say "check platform for current offers"
- Include a "last verified" date for promo codes
- Explain who each platform is NOT for
- Include step-by-step signup instructions
- Link to educational content for PM concept explanations
